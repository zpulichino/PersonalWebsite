September 2022

Scheduler IOS App

Spent five months working in the XCode IDE using Swift and SwiftUI to create an IOS Application. The app allowed for users to input their schedule for the month, and would then give them countdowns till their next event, as well as counting down how much time is left in their current event. The app was intergrated with Microsoft's Project Scheduling API's to help improve functionality. The app only ever reached the beta stage being release to close friends and family for critque.
